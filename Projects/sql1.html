Covid Information

  • This project aims to showcase the versatility and practicality of SQL in various database management scenarios.

  • In this project, we delve into Covid Information of all Countries and utilize the Power of Queries in SQL.

  • Death Percentage - We check the Total Cases vs Total Deaths.
    'Y' and 'N' to 'Yes' and 'No' in Sold Column.

  • Infection Rate - Infection Rate is checked by Country to Country bases showcasing the highest Infected Country at the top.
    Similarly we check for Death Rate

  • Vaccination Percentage - Total Population vs The no. of People who have Vaccinated and their Percentage.

  • CTE and Temp Tables - We use CTE and Temp Tabels to Reuse and Optimize the Queries

  • Views - We use Views for Simplifying and to Optimize the Queries
