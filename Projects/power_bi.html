Power Bi Project

  • Power BI offer enhanced interactivity, dynamic filtering, and integration with external data sources for a comprehensive and insightful analysis of the surveyed data.

  • More than 600 people were surveyed and based on their answers the following insights were taken from it.

  • Surveyors vs their Avg Salary - From the data Data Scientist made more all other job roles that were given.

  • Countries Surveyors Lived in - Most were from USA followed by other(encapsulates alot of countries together). India came in 3rd.

  • Favorite Programming Language - Python was by far people favorite programming language folowed by R, c/c++, JavaScript.

  • Average Salary by Gender - Women make just a little more avg salary than men from the people that we surveyed. 51-49%
